Delicious Beginnings

The amalgamation of emulation station, retroarch, and raspbian, the RetroPie gives you 50+ emulators of popular consoles, themes, and open source goodness. Mmmm, mmmm, good!

Since it's release in February of 2013, it's brought gamers all over the world together. Since then thousands of custom pies have been created.

List of Tasty Needs

To get up and running you'll need the following hardware:

  • Raspberry Pi (3 recommended)
  • 5V 2.5A Power Supply
  • 8GB Micro SD Card (minimum)
  • HDMI or RCA 4 Pole Cables
  • Gamepads and/or Keyboard
  • Ethernet or Wifi dongle
  • Raspberry Pi Case(optional)
  • Micro SD Card Reader

Bring It All Together!

Don't forget all the yummy code. Complete the installation by doing the following:

  • Find the RetroPie image that fits your Raspberry Pi at:RetroPie Home Page
  • Extract onto compatible SD card using Win32DiskImager
  • Turn on Raspberry Pie. Watch the magic happen! Doesn't it smell great?!

If your pie is undercooked, let us know!
