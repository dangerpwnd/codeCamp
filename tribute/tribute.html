The Creation of the D

The dynamic duo found each other in the mid 1980s touring together in an actor troupe.

Once they got past their petty diffences, they formed a band the likes of which have never been seen. Thats' right Tenacious D.

From playing around and learning each others strengths thru performance they came up with their first hit song. This song was just a tribute to the Metallica song One.

Just A Tribute

From their hubble beginnings, they went on to create multiple shorts for HBO as well as appearing on the comedy sketch show Mr.Show

They gained their permanent drummer Dave Grohl, formerly of Nirvana, and released their first self titled studio album in 2001. Their song "Tribute" gained cult-like status overseas as the band began to tour.

In 2003, they released their first concert DVD "The Complete Master Works", which went on to achieve gold and platinum status by the RIAA.

A Bigger D

After touring, the band decided to take a stab at the big screen, recording and staring in "The Pick of Destiny"

Having worked on the script since 2000, they began working with their friend and director Liam Lynch. The story foretold the fictional portryal of the band's formation.

Unfortunately the movie was a flop in the box office, and they quietly escaped the spotlight, playing venues sportaically until 2012.

Rize of the Fenix

Their "comeback" album was preceded by a mockumentary, titled "Tenacious D - To Be the Best", documented the uncertain future of the D after the box-office failure of The Pick of Destiny, KG's breakdown and incarceration in "an instituion", and Jack's embracing of an indulgent lifestyle.

On April 28,2012 the album was released in it's entirety on soundcloud where it received positive reviews from music critics.

Unplugged and Unprotected

Since their last release, the band has completed multiple world tours and has hinted at their next masterpiece.

Called "Post-Apocalypto" it is said to be released in the very near future.

Will the world be ready?
